Lamborghini Miura

Discover the Legend

Unleash the captivating tale of the Lamborghini Miura, an automotive masterpiece that forever altered the world of cars. With its central V12 engine and a breathtaking body design, this two-seater from the iconic Italian car manufacturer redefined the very essence of a sports car.

Embark on a journey with the Miura as it burst into the world, seizing the title of the fastest production car ever built. Blazing from 0 to 100 km/h in a mere 6.7 seconds and boasting an astonishing top speed of 280 km/h, the Lamborghini Miura redefined the very concept of speed and performance in the realm of sports cars.

Legacy of Excellence

Behold the legacy of the Miura, where 475 units of the 350 CV beauty were meticulously crafted, adorned with the unmistakable and captivating "eyelashes." This feat of automotive engineering evolved further, giving rise to the Miura S with 370 CV, and eventually, the Miura SV. The SV boasted wider mudguards and a power-packed 385 CV engine, complete with separate lubrication systems for both the engine and gearbox.
